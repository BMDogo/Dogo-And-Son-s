WELcome to Dogo & Sons Rice Farm

Growing Quality Rice for Every Home.

For over 20 years, Dogo & Sons Rice Farm has combined tradition and modern farming technology to produce clean, healthy, premium rice for families and businesses across Nigeria.

About Us

Dogo & Sons Rice Farm is a family-driven agricultural company with over two decades of experience in rice cultivation and processing. We began as a small family farm and have grown into a modern agribusiness with tractors, irrigation systems, and a dedicated processing unit.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To produce clean, high-quality rice using modern technology while preserving our farming heritage.

We grow rice during both the rainy season and the dry season, allowing us to produce fresh grains all year round.

Our-Story

Our story began more than 20 years ago with a single vision: to create a reliable rice farm that produces quality grains for the community. What started with simple tools and manual labour has now evolved into a fully mechanized operation. With tractors, harvesters, irrigation tools, nursery trays, and processing machines, we continue to modernize while staying rooted in our family values. Dogo & Sons is not just a farm it’s a legacy, a dream, and a commitment passed from one generation to the next.

Farming Process

We follow a clean, modern farming process to ensure premium rice quality:

Land Preparation using tractors

Seed Selection & Nursery Development

Transplanting / Mechanized Seeding

Smart Irrigation System (dry-season farming)

Crop Monitoring with Drones

Modern Fertilization and Weed Control

Harvesting with Machines

Drying and Cleaning

Processing & Milling

Packaging into Branded Sacks

Our commitment is to produce rice that is healthy, clean, and affordable.
